### Known Limitations (Cannot Be Proven Headlessly)
1. **APK runtime behavior**: Headless validation cannot prove the APK actually runs on an Android device. The APK structure is valid (manifest, dex, libs) but actual execution requires device testing.
2. **Touch input**: Virtual joystick and attack button touch responsiveness cannot be validated headlessly. Physical device testing required.
3. **Gameplay loop timing**: NavigationAgent3D pathfinding recalculation timing, enemy AI decision cycles, and wave transition timing require device testing.
4. **Audio playback**: Procedural AudioStreamGenerator output cannot be validated headlessly without actual audio hardware.

1. The debug keystore at `/home/rowan/.android/debug.keystore` is valid for APK signing (ANDROID-001 confirmed)
