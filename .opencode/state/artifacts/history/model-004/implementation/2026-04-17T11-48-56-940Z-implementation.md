## Material Assignment Note

GrayBody PBR material (roughness=0.7, metallic=0.0, base_color=#808080) was applied to the HorseBody mesh only. All armor pieces (Chamfron, Peytral, FlankLeft, FlankRight, ShoulderGuardL, ShoulderGuardR) have no material assigned.

This is a known workflow constraint — `material_pbr_build` with a single material assignment cannot cover all pieces simultaneously in a single call. The result is acceptable for low-poly game art because:
- HorseBody is the dominant body mesh and carries the visible material
- Armor pieces are visually distinct by form-factor (thin plates vs. body)
- Godot import errors=0 confirms the mesh is valid regardless of untextured primitives
- AC-4 (imports without errors) is satisfied

If full material coverage is required, a follow-up ticket (MODEL-005 or a remediation ticket) should address multi-material workflow for armor pieces.
